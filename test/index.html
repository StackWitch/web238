My Semester Project

Topic

I am creating a website that helps calculate what time you need to leave to go to the airport. The goal is to keep it clean, simple, and easy to use.

Inspiration Website

My inspiration is Google Flights. I like the minimal, uncluttered design, the simple interface, and the way it guides the user without confusion. I plan to bring those same qualities into my project.
